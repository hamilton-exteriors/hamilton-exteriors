When drafting review responses, rotate through these cities and services so no single keyword dominates. Track which ones were used in recent responses to maintain good coverage.

## Rotation Rules

1. Never use the same city in back-to-back responses
2. Never use the same service keyword in back-to-back responses
3. If the reviewer mentions their city or project type, use their words (most natural)
4. If the reviewer doesn't mention location, pick the next city in the rotation
5. Aim for coverage: over 10 responses, try to hit at least 5 different cities and 3 different services
6. Short reviews (1-2 sentences) may skip keywords entirely — don't force it

## Our Oakland Services

### Roofing in Oakland

Oakland's climate and fire risk make roofing a specialized trade here. Homes in the Oakland Hills and Berkeley Hills fall within Wildland-Urban Interface (WUI) Local Responsibility Areas, as designated by [CAL FIRE](https://www.fire.ca.gov). That means Class A fire-rated roofing materials are required, along with specific underlayment, edge metal, and sometimes venting modifications.

We install asphalt shingles, metal roofing, tile, and flat roofing systems. For a typical 2,000-square-foot home with architectural asphalt shingles, Oakland homeowners can expect to pay $18,800 to $22,500, including tear-off, underlayment, flashing, and permit fees of $300 to $650 ([Oakland Building & Safety](https://www.oaklandca.gov/topics/permits-and-construction)). Homes in Rockridge and Glenview frequently need decking repair under the shingles, which adds $1,000 to $3,000 depending on the extent of rot.

## Oakland-Specific Considerations

### Fire Risk in the Oakland Hills

The Oakland Hills fire zone requires Class A fire-rated roofing materials, ember-resistant vents, and defensible space compliance. Homes in the WUI Local Responsibility Area must follow California Chapter 7A standards. We install GAF Timberline HDZ shingles with a Class A fire rating and 130 mph wind warranty, and we can advise on metal roofing for maximum fire resistance.

### Seismic Considerations

Oakland sits near the Hayward Fault, one of the most active fault zones in California. Any addition or major renovation that modifies the roof or foundation will require structural tie-down inspections. We work with structural engineers who specialize in Bay Area seismic retrofitting and include reinforcement costs in our estimates.

### Climate and Corrosion

Oakland's coastal fog carries salt-laden moisture that accelerates corrosion on exposed metal flashing, gutters, and fasteners. We use stainless steel fasteners and corrosion-resistant flashing on every Oakland project. For homes in the flatlands near the estuary, we recommend upgrading to premium synthetic underlayment and standing-seam metal roofing for maximum longevity.
